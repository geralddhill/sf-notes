# JP

## Portal Game

- When he puts portal up fullscreen, respect it; try to inch forward after blocking.
- When he puts portal up in neutral range, drive rush and punish him for it (don't let him get it up for free)

## Fireball Game
- OD fireball more to go through JP's fireballs

Try walking past portals and using burn knuckle

In the corner against Level 2 on block, drive reversal is your only option
