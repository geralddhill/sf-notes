# High Low

## Fireball/Tiger Knee

Tiger knee is more plus when its blocked low, so he conditions you to block low by throwing high fireball

## Tiger Nexus

Light followup is -5 but uninterruptable and blocked high. Med and heavy are interruptable (jab and c.MP punishable respectively) but are plus when blocked high.

# Footsies/Ranges

Stand fierce isn't special cancellable, so it's less punishable to get hit by it. Crouching fierce is special cancellable, so you don't want to get hit by it. Because stand fierce isn't too bad to get hit by, you can stay in that range, but you want to stay out of crouch fierce range.
