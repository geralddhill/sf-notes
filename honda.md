# Honda

Throwing is good against Honda, so establish the throw shimmy mixup early.

## Fireball Game
- No L Fireball
- M fireball is good but only in the med range (where it hits)

## Buttslam
- Be aware of his down charge
    - i.e. if he walks forward, you don't have to worry about buttslam
- +1 is sometimes fake (dependent on spacing)

Be careful of neutral jump in disadvantage

Don't get tilted off command grab (it's not that much damage)
